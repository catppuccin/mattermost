## Usage

1. Open Mattermost.
2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Display** > **Theme** > **Edit** > **Custom Theme**.
3. Copy the contents of your preferred flavor from [`themes/`](./themes/) into the "Copy and paste to share theme colors" section.
